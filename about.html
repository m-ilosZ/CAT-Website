About Climate Action Team

The Climate Action Team (CAT) is a student-led initiative where high school students plan and execute environmental programs month by month. Managing a $10,000 Portland Green Energy Fund grant, our students organize everything from outreach workshops to community events, farm visits, and sustainable transportation initiatives (so far), creating real impact in our community.
