February: Youth Education

This branch of the CAT club's goal is to spread climate change awareness to the next generation of community members. We aim to inform and empower kids in middle and elementary schools in our area to make real change. Through the months of March to June, a small team of CAT students will travel to as many Grant feeder schools as possible. We will give an interactive presentation written by our students, for students. The CAT is reaching out as much as we can, but if you would like us to come to your school please let us know at pdavis3957@student.pps.net or info@cateam.tech
